An indexed set of vectors \(S = \set{\vec{v}_1, \dots, \vec{v}_p}\) in \(\mathbb{R}^n\) (where \(p \geq 2\)) is linearly dependent if and only if at least one of the vectors in \(S\) can be written as a linear combination of the others.
