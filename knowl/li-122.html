Item 3.
For row replacement, say \(R_2 + k R_1 \rightarrow R_2\text{,}\)
\begin{align*} \det{\begin{bmatrix} a \amp b \\ c + ka \amp d + kb \end{bmatrix}} \amp = a(d + kb) - b(c + ka)\\ \amp = ad - bc + abk - abk\\ \amp = ad - bc = \det{A} \end{align*}
This row operation doesn't change the value of the determinant.
