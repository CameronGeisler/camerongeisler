The term vector is used widely in mathematics and physics. Here, a vector will simply mean an ordered list of real numbers. In linear algebra, we represent vectors most commonly as a column matrix, a matrix with dimensions \(1 \times n\text{,}\) i.e. a matrix with 1 row and \(n\) “columns”. Similarly, a row vector is a \(m \times 1\) matrix, a matrix which has 1 columns and \(m\) “rows”.
