Definition 6.1.7.

For a collection of vectors \(\vec{v}_1, \dots, \vec{v}_p \in \mathbb{R}^n\text{,}\) their span \(\Span{\vec{v}_1, \dots, \vec{v}_p}\) is a subspace of \(\mathbb{R}^n\text{.}\) Then, \(\Span{\vec{v}_1, \dots, \vec{v}_p}\) is called the subspace spanned by \(\vec{v}_1, \dots, \vec{v}_p\text{.}\)
