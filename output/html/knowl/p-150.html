Another method of specifying a line is to specify two points \(\vec{r}_0, \vec{r}_1\) on the line. This is because a direction vector for the line is the difference between the points, \(\vec{v} = \vec{r}_1 - \vec{r}_0\text{,}\) and one of the points (say \(\vec{r}_0\)) can be used as the starting point.
